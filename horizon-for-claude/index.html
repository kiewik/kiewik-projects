Horizon for Claude 2.1.19

Understand Claude’s limits at a glance.

A free Chrome extension that combines 5-hour and weekly usage, reset times, pace and forecasts directly on Claude.ai.

Privacy by design

Horizon does not read prompts or conversation content. Usage readings, history and forecasts are handled locally in Chrome. Settings may use Chrome Sync when enabled by the user.
